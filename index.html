About Us

Chicken N Rice is a family-owned and operated restaurant that has been serving up delicious chicken and rice dishes for over 20 years. We use only the freshest ingredients and our chicken is always cooked to perfection. Our menu features a variety of chicken dishes to choose from, as well as sides and desserts. We also offer a variety of catering options for all occasions.
